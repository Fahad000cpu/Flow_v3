# **App Name**: LinkShare

## Core Features:

- Product Feed: Personalized product feed: Displaying relevant product recommendations to the user based on their social network activity, purchase history, and indicated preferences.
- User Profiles: User profiles: Profiles showcasing each user's style, saved products, and recommendations they've shared with their network.
- In-App Purchasing: Direct in-app purchasing: Seamless and secure purchasing process for products featured in the app.
- Social Interaction: Social interaction: Like, save, and comment on shared products to increase user engagement. Users can also create product collections.
- AI Product Curator: AI Product Curator Tool: Curate products using AI that identifies current trends and recommends products with similar features to other user's wishlists.
- Easy Share: Easy share links: Links can easily be copied or shared through SMS and email

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft pastel purple (#D0BFFF) to evoke a sense of creativity and community.
- Background color: Very light pastel purple (#F8F7FF) to maintain a light and airy feel.
- Accent color: Muted teal (#A0D2EB) to add a touch of calmness and trust.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for a clean and modern reading experience.
- Simple and outlined icons for product categories, user actions, and sharing options.
- Clean and user-friendly layouts for the product feed and the profiles.
